Akagera Motors Rwanda was founded in Kigali in 2010 with a single mission: make it easy for Rwandans to access the world's finest vehicles at fair, transparent prices. From our flagship showroom in the heart of Kigali, we have grown to become the country's most trusted new-car dealership.

We partner directly with manufacturers across Japan, Europe, China, and the United States to ensure every vehicle we sell is brand new, fully documented, and delivered with the manufacturer's warranty intact. Our team speaks Kinyarwanda, French, English, and Swahili.

As Rwanda accelerates towards a greener economy, we are proud to lead the charge in electric vehicle adoption. Our BYD, Tesla, and MG selection is the widest in the country, backed by certified EV specialists.
